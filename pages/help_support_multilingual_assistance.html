Common Issues

സാധാരണ പ്രശ്നങ്ങൾ

Camera not working

Check camera permissions in your phone settings. Go to Settings > Apps > AgriAssist > Permissions > Camera

Voice recognition issues

Ensure microphone permissions are enabled. Speak clearly and reduce background noise for better recognition.

Slow loading in rural areas

AgriAssist is optimized for 2G/3G networks. Enable offline mode for faster access to essential features.

Step-by-step Guides

3
