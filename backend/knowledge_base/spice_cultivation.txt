Spice Cultivation in Kerala - Complete Guide

MAJOR SPICES IN KERALA:
- Black Pepper (കുരുമുളക്)
- Cardamom (ഏലം)
- Cinnamon (കറുവ)
- Clove (കരയാമ്പൂ)
- Nutmeg (ജാതിക്ക)
- Turmeric (മഞ്ഞൾ)
- Ginger (ഇഞ്ചി)
- Vanilla (വാനില)
- Star Anise (തകോലം)

BLACK PEPPER CULTIVATION:
- Best time: May-June
- Spacing: 3m x 3m
- Support: Live support trees (Erythrina, Silver Oak)
- Training: Train vines on support
- Harvesting: 3-4 years after planting
- Yield: 2-3 kg per vine per year

CARDAMOM CULTIVATION:
- Altitude: 600-1200m above sea level
- Soil: Well-drained, rich in organic matter
- Planting: June-July
- Spacing: 2m x 2m
- Shade: 50-60% shade required
- Harvesting: October-December
- Processing: Sun drying for 3-4 days

TURMERIC CULTIVATION:
- Planting: April-May
- Spacing: 30cm x 30cm
- Soil: Sandy loam, good drainage
- Watering: Regular, avoid waterlogging
- Harvesting: 7-9 months after planting
- Processing: Boiling, sun drying, polishing

GINGER CULTIVATION:
- Planting: April-May
- Spacing: 25cm x 25cm
- Soil: Well-drained, rich in organic matter
- Mulching: Use dry leaves or straw
- Harvesting: 8-10 months after planting
- Storage: Store in cool, dry place

COMMON PESTS AND DISEASES:
- Thrips: Neem oil spray
- Scale insects: Malathion spray
- Root rot: Proper drainage
- Leaf spot: Copper fungicide
- Bacterial wilt: Crop rotation

ORGANIC PEST CONTROL:
- Neem oil: 2ml per liter water
- Garlic extract: 10g per liter water
- Chili extract: 5g per liter water
- Cow urine: 1:10 dilution

FERTILIZER REQUIREMENTS:
- Organic: FYM 10-15 tons/hectare
- NPK: 100:50:100 kg/hectare
- Micronutrients: Zinc, Boron, Manganese
- Foliar spray: 19:19:19 NPK

MARKETING TIPS:
- Grade spices properly
- Clean and dry thoroughly
- Store in airtight containers
- Get organic certification
- Direct marketing to exporters

CLIMATE REQUIREMENTS:
- Temperature: 20-30°C
- Rainfall: 1500-3000mm annually
- Humidity: 70-80%
- Wind: Moderate, not strong winds
